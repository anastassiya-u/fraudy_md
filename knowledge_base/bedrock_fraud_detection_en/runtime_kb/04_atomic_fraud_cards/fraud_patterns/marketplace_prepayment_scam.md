# Marketplace prepayment scam

## Pattern summary
A buyer or seller moves the transaction outside the marketplace and requests prepayment, courier payment, or card verification.

## Core deception
The user is led to believe an external payment/courier link is required to reserve, receive, or release funds.

## Common channels
- marketplace
- messenger
- sms
- web_page

## Red flags
- External payment or delivery link
- Card details requested to receive money
- Urgency to confirm reservation or courier pickup
- Refusal to use marketplace payment tools

## Scam examples
- Buyer paid; enter your card to receive funds at [unknown-link].
- Reserve item now with prepayment outside the platform.

## Legitimate contrast
Legitimate marketplace transactions usually keep payment and dispute protection inside the platform.

## What NOT to classify as fraud automatically
- Negotiation inside platform with no external payment link
- Cash-on-delivery or platform escrow terms are clear and verified

## Safe user actions
- Keep payment inside the marketplace
- Do not enter card data to receive money
- Avoid external courier/payment links
- Report suspicious account to platform

## Runtime model instruction
Use this card for classifieds and marketplace chats. Increase risk when external payment links or card-to-receive-money patterns appear.
