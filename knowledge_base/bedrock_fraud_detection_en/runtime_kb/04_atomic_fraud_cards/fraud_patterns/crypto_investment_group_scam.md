# Crypto investment group scam

## Pattern summary
A social-media or messaging group promotes guaranteed crypto/investment returns and pushes deposits to a platform or wallet.

## Core deception
The user is led to believe profits are guaranteed and visible dashboard gains are withdrawable after fees.

## Common channels
- messenger
- social_media
- web_page
- email

## Red flags
- Guaranteed or unusually high returns
- Pressure to deposit more
- Withdrawal blocked until tax/fee is paid
- Private key or seed phrase requested
- Fake dashboard profits

## Scam examples
- Join our crypto group for guaranteed daily returns.
- Pay withdrawal tax to release your profit.

## Legitimate contrast
Legitimate investing involves risk disclosure, regulated entities, and no guaranteed profit or withdrawal fee paid to a random contact.

## What NOT to classify as fraud automatically
- User is reviewing a regulated broker independently
- No deposit pressure or guaranteed returns are present

## Safe user actions
- Stop deposits
- Do not pay withdrawal fees
- Never share seed phrase/private keys
- Preserve chats and transaction IDs

## Runtime model instruction
Use this card when investment advice comes from strangers/groups and includes guaranteed returns, blocked withdrawals, or fee escalation.
