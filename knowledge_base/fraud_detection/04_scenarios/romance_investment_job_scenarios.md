# Romance, investment, and job scam scenarios

## Romance scam progression

1. Rapid personal attention and emotional bonding.
2. Excuses to avoid in-person/video verification.
3. Crisis, travel, medical, customs, or family emergency story.
4. Request for money, gift cards, crypto, or receiving/transferring funds.
5. Continued pressure, secrecy, and escalation.

## Investment scam progression

1. Ad, influencer, stranger, or romantic contact promises profit.
2. User is moved to platform or broker outside regulated channels.
3. Fake dashboard shows profits.
4. Withdrawal requires tax, fee, verification payment, or bigger deposit.
5. Support disappears or account is frozen.

## Job scam progression

1. Attractive remote role with high pay and low requirements.
2. Vague company identity and fast hiring.
3. Move to messenger.
4. Upfront fee, training fee, equipment fee, task deposit, or card/bank data request.
5. Payment theft or identity theft.

## Critical rules

- Never pay money to receive a job, prize, withdrawal, refund, or recovered funds.
- Financial advice from a new online relationship is high risk.
- A fake dashboard is not proof of real profit.
