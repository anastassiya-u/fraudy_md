# Job scam datasets

## Use cases

- Detect fake job postings and employment scam patterns.
- Compare legitimate recruitment messages with scam offers.
- Build features for unrealistic compensation, vague duties, and upfront payment requests.

## Key indicators

- High salary with low requirements and vague company details.
- Upfront registration/training/equipment fee.
- “Task deposit” or pay-to-work model.
- Request for bank/card details before contract and identity verification.
- Movement from job platform/email to private messenger without legitimate reason.
- Email domain does not match company website.

## Benign controls

Legitimate recruiters may use messengers or ask for documents after interview stages. Risk becomes high when payment, deposit, card details, or suspicious identity documents are requested before formal employment.
